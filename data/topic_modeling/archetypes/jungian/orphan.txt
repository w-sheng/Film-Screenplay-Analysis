This archetype embodies the good old boy, the girl next door, or the average Joe. The Everyperson is down to earth and connects with others through his or her humility. Many main characters are Everyman archetypes because this person is someone that we can all relate to. Bilbo Baggins is an Everyperson. Sometimes, the Everyperson is a sidekick, such as Ron Weasley from Harry Potter.
